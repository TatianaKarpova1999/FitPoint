Privacy Policy - FitPoint

Introduction

Welcome to FitPoint. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information while providing a safe and high-quality fitness experience.

Data Collection and Usage

FitPoint collects only the necessary information to enhance your membership experience, including your contact details, fitness preferences, and activity data. This information helps us personalize your training programs and improve our services.

Security and Data Protection

We implement advanced security measures, including encryption and secure storage, to protect your personal data. Your information is never shared with third parties without your explicit consent.

User Rights and Preferences

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal information at any time. Our platform allows you to manage your privacy settings easily to ensure transparency and control over your data.

Membership and Community Standards

FitPoint is dedicated to maintaining a safe and respectful environment for all members. We enforce strict policies to ensure fair access to our facilities and a positive gym experience.

Support and Contact

If you have any privacy concerns or questions, please reach out to us at contact@fitpoint.com

Compliance and Data Protection

FitPoint complies with all relevant data protection laws, including GDPR and CCPA, ensuring that your data is handled securely and responsibly.
