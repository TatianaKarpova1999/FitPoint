Privacy Policy - StarScreen

Introduction

Welcome to StarScreen. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information while providing a seamless and enjoyable streaming experience.

Data Collection and Usage

StarScreen collects only the necessary information to enhance your viewing experience, including your contact details, viewing history, preferences, and device information. This data helps us personalize recommendations and improve our services.

Security and Data Protection

We implement advanced security measures, including encryption and secure storage, to protect your personal data. Your information is never shared with third parties without your explicit consent.

User Rights and Preferences

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal information at any time. Our platform allows you to manage your privacy settings easily, ensuring transparency and control over your data.

Streaming and Community Standards

StarScreen is dedicated to maintaining a safe and respectful environment for all users. We enforce strict policies to prevent unauthorized content sharing and ensure a positive streaming experience for everyone.

Support and Contact

If you have any privacy concerns or questions, please reach out to us at contact@starscreen.com

Compliance and Data Protection

StarScreen complies with all relevant data protection laws, including GDPR and CCPA, ensuring that your data is handled securely and responsibly.
