Frequently asked questions

What facilities are available at FitnessPoint?

FitnessPoint offers a fully equipped gym with the latest cardio and strength training equipment, a wide variety of machines, free weights, and an area for stretching and functional workouts. We also have a spacious, clean, and comfortable environment for our members.

Do I need to bring my own equipment to workout at FitnessPoint?

No, FitnessPoint provides all the equipment you'll need for your workouts, including weights, cardio machines, mats, and more. You can focus on your workout without worrying about bringing extra gear.

Can I book training sessions through the app?

Absolutely! You can book your personal training sessions directly through the FitnessPoint app. Youâ€™ll also get reminders and updates about your upcoming appointments.

Can I track my workout progress in the app?

Yes, the app allows you to track your workouts, log your progress, and even set goals. You can monitor your performance over time and make adjustments to your fitness routine accordingly.

Can I bring a friend to FitnessPoint?

Yes, members can bring a guest to FitnessPoint. Please check with the front desk for our guest policy and any associated fees.

What features are available in the FitnessPoint app?

The FitnessPoint app provides access to your workout schedules, progress tracking, nutrition plans, class bookings, and more. You can also receive personalized recommendations and track your fitness goals right from the app.

Do you offer fitness classes or personal training at FitnessPoint?

Yes, FitnessPoint offers a variety of fitness classes and personal training services. Our experienced trainers can provide one-on-one coaching or group fitness classes to help you achieve your fitness goals.
