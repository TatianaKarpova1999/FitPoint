Frequently Asked Questions

What is StarScreen?

StarScreen is an online streaming platform offering a vast library of movies, TV shows, and exclusive content in high definition. Watch anywhere, anytime on multiple devices.

How can I subscribe to StarScreen?

You can subscribe by visiting our website, selecting a subscription plan that suits you, and creating an account. Payment can be made via credit card, PayPal, or other secure payment methods.

Can I watch StarScreen on multiple devices?

Yes! StarScreen is available on smartphones, tablets, smart TVs, and web browsers. You can also download the app for offline viewing.

Does StarScreen offer a free trial?

Yes, we offer a free trial for new users. You can explore our content library before deciding on a subscription plan.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Absolutely! You can cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings, and you will still have access until the end of your billing cycle.

Does StarScreen have exclusive movies and series?

Yes! StarScreen offers a selection of exclusive movies and series you won’t find anywhere else. Stay tuned for new releases every month.

What internet speed do I need to stream on StarScreen?

For SD streaming, you need at least 3 Mbps, for HD 5 Mbps, and for 4K Ultra HD, we recommend at least 25 Mbps.
