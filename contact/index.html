Frequently asked questions

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team via email, phone, or live chat on our website. Our team is available 24/7 to assist you.

How do I contact my personal trainer?

You can contact your personal trainer through the training app's messaging feature or by email. If you need urgent assistance, please check their availability in the app.

Can I schedule a call with my trainer?

Yes! You can schedule a call with your trainer through the app or by contacting them directly. Many trainers offer virtual consultations for additional support.

What if I don’t receive a response from support?

If you don’t receive a response within 24 hours, please try reaching out via another contact method, such as phone or live chat. We aim to respond as quickly as possible.
